--- layout: post title: Developer Toolbar for IE date: '2005-09-19T12:04:00.000+10:00' author: Richard Banks tags: modified_time: '2005-09-19T12:07:04.773+10:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-13321238.post-112709549982848678 blogger_orig_url: http://www.richard-banks.org/2005/09/developer-toolbar-for-ie.html --- The IE team has released a developers toolbar for IE that makes a lot of nifty features available for developers.

The story is on the IEBlog website and the download is available as well.

Some features that I really like
- DOM explorer (as a pinnable toolbar inside IE)
- Highlighting of divs, tables, etc
- Inbuilt ruler for measuring distances
- Preconfigured windows resizing option

Really nice stuff :-)
