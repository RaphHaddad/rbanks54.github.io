--- layout: post title: Putting the AtomsFramework on hold (for now) date: '2005-07-26T07:40:00.000+10:00' author: Richard Banks tags: modified_time: '2005-07-26T07:53:30.513+10:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-13321238.post-112232841050727478 blogger_orig_url: http://www.richard-banks.org/2005/07/putting-atomsframework-on-hold-for-now.html --- I've decided to put the AtomsFramework (the open source object/relational persistence framework) on hold for the next 6 months or so, and to then decide what to do with it after that.

This has been a difficult decision, since there is a lot of pride and effort invested in the software, but in my new role as the development manager for a rapidly expanding software company, I've found that there are a number of large problems I've must devote my time and energy to, and this means that having time for an open source project would mean I'd be sacrificing the time I spend with my family.

In the mean time, if there are any volunteers who wish to take up the baton and keep the AtomsFramework moving forward, please let me know. Alternatively, have a look at some of the other open source O/R frameworks such as NHibernate or Gentle.NET.
