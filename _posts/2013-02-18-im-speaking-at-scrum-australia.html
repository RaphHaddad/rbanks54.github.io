The inaugural Scrum Australia conference is on in Sydney this April and I’ll be there, speaking about the mistakes Product Owners tend to make.  if that doesn’t interest you then fear not, as there’s plenty of other great content being delivered. Don’t believe me? Just have a look at the schedule. There’s even an open space event for making sure you get to talk with other people about topics that specifically interest you.

This should be a great conference so talk to work and arrange some time out so you can get along on April 11 and 12. I’ll see you there!
